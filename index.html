The framework

The conversions that actually move enrollment.

Five stages, five conversion rates. If you don't know all five for your school, you don't know where you're losing students. We do — and we coach the people on your team who can move each one.

  1. 01

    Lead → Contact

    Reach them.

    How fast you call back, what you say in the first fifteen seconds, and whether you keep the conversation alive long enough to ask for a tour.

    →
  2. 02

    Contact → Schedule

    Earn the tour.

    Asking for the campus visit without sounding pushy. Handling "just looking" and "I need to think about it" the way a coach handles them.

    →
  3. 03

    Schedule → Tour

    Get them through the door.

    Confirmation calls, reminder texts, the day-before. Cutting no-shows from a tour problem into a conversion problem.

    →
  4. 04

    Tour → Enroll

    Close the tour.

    Pacing the walk-through, telling the school's story, the questions you ask, when you bring up tuition, and how you ask for the deposit.

    →
  5. 05

    Enroll → Start

    Keep them committed.

    The window between enrollment and day one is where cold feet happen. We coach the follow-up that gets them showing up.
